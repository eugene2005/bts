    Cancelled gigs, quarantine, and fear of Coronavirus, force boy band BTS to call it quits, Big Hit Entertainment announced through their Twitter account.

    Holly Gordon · CBC Music · Posted: Apr 01, 2020 3:46 AM ET | Last Updated: 4 hours ago

    Seven members of South Korean K-Pop band V, Jungkook, Jimin, SUGA, Jin, RM, and J-Hope decided to part their ways after 7 years of successful entertainment career. World spread pandemic of COVID-19 has hit hard the entertainment industry. Amongst many other bands, BTS had to cancel their upcoming shows, be placed in quarantine, which prohibits gethering of more than five people, so not all seven band members can perform together at any one time. Additionally, two of the memebers have decided to use the down time to complete military service, which is still mandatory in South Korea.

    BTS leaves behind "A.R.M.Y" - millions of fans around the world, who will surely be devastated by the news.

    At this time of financial hardship for all musicians around the world, it is important to support your great local musicians like Celine Dion, Brian Adams, and Justin Bieber.
